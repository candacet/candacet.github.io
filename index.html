Instant Pot Buffalo Chicken Dip

  • Time: 23 mins
  • Serving Size: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1 lb.
Ranch 3/4 cup
Hot Sauce 1 cup
Colby Jack Cheese 3/4 cup
Cheddar Cheese 3/4 cup
Cream Cheese 8 oz.

Step 1: Put the ranch, hot sauce and chicken in the Instant Pot for 10 minutes on high pressure.
Step 2: Let pressure release naturally for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes manually release.
Step 3: Using two forks, shred the chicken.
Step 4: Add the cream cheese and shredded cheese (mix).
Step 5: Broil for 5 minutes.

Quinoa Spinach Stuffed Tomatoes

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serving Size: 8-10
Ingredients Quantity
Quinoa 1 cup
Water 2 cups
Tomatoes 8 medium
Spinach 6 cups
Mozz. Cheese Handful
Salt/Pepper Just a pinch

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375.
Step 2: Slice off 1/2 inch of the stem end of the tomatoes and hollow out the inside.
Step 3: Slice just a small section off of the bottom of the tomatoes so that they will sit flat on a baking sheet.
Step 4: Sprinkle salt in the hollow portion of each tomato and place hollow side up on a baking sheet. (Set Aside)
Step 5: Cook quinoa and spinach.
Step 6: Evenly divide the filling among the tomatoes.
Step 7: Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
Step 8: Remove foil, sprinkle tops with mozzarella cheese and bake for 5 minutes.

Homemade Vanilla Coffee Syrup

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serving Size: 1 jar
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1 3/4 cups
Granulated Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Light Brown Sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla Bean 1 (split in half)
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp

Step 1: In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, whisk together water, sugar, and vanilla bean.
Step 2: Bring to a boil and reduce heat.
Step 3: Simmer for about five minutes - the sugar should be completely dissolved.
Step 4: Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
Step 5: Once simple syrup has cooled, remove the vanilla bean and pour the syrup into a glass jar or bottle.

*Store in refrigerator*

Source: My Baking Addiction
